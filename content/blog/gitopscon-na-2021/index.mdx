The GitOps Working Group is proud to announce that
the schedule for GitOpsCon North America 2021 has been
released! You can see the full schedule on the [official event website](https://events.linuxfoundation.org/gitopscon-north-america/program/schedule/).

We like to give a special thanks to our diamond level sponsors [Amazon
(AWS)](https://aws.amazon.com/), [Red Hat](https://www.redhat.com),
and [Weaveworks](https://www.weave.works). We also like to thank
our Gold level sponsors [Codefresh](https://codefresh.io/) and
[HashiCorp](https://www.hashicorp.com/) as well. You all rock!

GitOpsCon will have talks aimed at every level. So whether you are
just begining your GitOps journey, or are a current user of GitOps,
you will find a great deal of information. From end user stories to how
tos, there is plenty of content to keep you interested.

Come join us as we hear talks from by Chick-fil-A, CERN, State Farm,
Starbucks, and many more about how GitOps is shaping their
environment. You will also hear from Amazon (AWS), Codefresh, Red Hat,
Weaveworks, and the other GitOps Working Group members.

This will be a Day 0 event Co-located with KubeCon. So when you are
registering for KubeCon, don't forget to add GitOpsCon! You can also
register for the event directly from the event page by clicking the
link below.
